What’s Caper?

Caper is a mood-based dish discovery app that allows people to find and purchase exciting food from hand-selected purveyors*


*Caper purveyors are locally-owned restaurants with a compelling story, memorable meals, and quality ingredients

How It Works

Your Mood

Pick your mood and we’ll give you four dishes near you, hand-selected by us and all under $15. No more overwhelming menus to sift through. No sub-par ingredients. No overpriced portions.

Their Story

If you're always looking for small locally-owned shops, you're in luck. We share these amazing plates and the incredible people behind them with you because the best meals are backed by great stories.

Meet Up & Eat Up

After a quick walk, your meal will be ready for pick-up by the time you arrive. Say goodbye to long waits, lost orders, late deliveries, and depressingly soggy, spilled, or lukewarm food.
