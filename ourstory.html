It all started with

A SIMPLE IDEA

Passionate chefs should have a platform to share their stories.

It began with a few food lovers, weary from late deliveries, overpriced portions, and a sea of underwhelming menu options. After realizing their best meals came from chefs with compelling (and often overlooked) food stories, they embarked on a journey to find an easier way to discover and eat new, exciting food.

And so, Caper was born.

Caper revolutionizes dish discovery to find food you’re in the mood for. We believe quality meals in NYC should be easy to find, affordable, and a short walk away. We also love small, local businesses and want them to succeed. Through Caper, diners can quickly find delicious eats that satisfy their most adventurous cravings and build traction for these restaurants. Purveyors* can now share the full richness of their craft while growing a community of diners. It’s a win-win.

*Caper purveyors are locally-owned restaurants with a compelling story, memorable meals, and quality ingredients
