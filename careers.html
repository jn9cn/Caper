Careers

Who We Are

We’re a food-technology startup revolutionizing dish-discovery and helping users find food they’re in the mood for. Our team is small, driven, diverse, and passionate. We all love to eat and learn about the people behind the plate too.

At Caper, we seek out adventurous eaters who enjoy trying new dishes. We connect food lovers with local purveyors and home chefs by sharing their inspiration behind the food. Caper purveyors come in all shapes and sizes — they’re the mom-and-pop store on the corner of the block, or the small hole-in-wall restaurant you love and jealously guard, or your great-aunt who makes a killer pound cake. We’re always on the lookout for new purveyors with interesting stories who can also dish up delicious meals in our target neighborhoods.

What We’re Looking For

  • Experience selling and prospecting to new customers or a willingness to learn
  • Ability to strategically think and analyze the needs of purveyors
  • Excellent verbal, written, and interpersonal communication skills
  • Quality editing abilities
  • Organized, detail-oriented, and a self-starter
  • Experience in a startup environment or business related field is a plus
  • Applicants will be based in our New York City offices
  • Flexibility in managing responsibilities - as this is a rapidly-growing and changing startup, duties and responsibilities may also evolve and change
  • Drive and desire to learn, grow, and take constructive feedback
  • Must love food!

Want to work (and eat) with us?
Drop us a line at info@caper.ly, We look forward to hearing from you.
